Company Engagement Program

Our Collection Agents and Field Collection Agents have the opportunity to earn monthly rewards, namely The Best Collection Agent and The Best Field Collection Agent.

These rewards are given as a form of appreciation for the high dedication and loyalty our agents have shown to the company.

The rewards are provided in the form of cash incentives and certificates, which will be beneficial for our agents in the future.

Our People Development

As part of our commitment to operational excellence, every new agent at VJR undergoes comprehensive training followed by supervised tandem sessions prior to deployment.

This approach ensures full readiness, capability, and alignment with our operational standards.

In addition, existing agents receive periodic refresher trainings to reinforce consistent adherence to SOPs, maintain performance quality, and ensure compliance with evolving regulatory requirements.
